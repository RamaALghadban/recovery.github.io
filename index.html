WHAT’S OUR USP:

We incorporate a variety of highly effective and sequential gold standard manual
therapy interventions to help prepare people for activity and maximise their recovery post
activity. Our performance & Recovery Specialists are Athletes with professional experience,
Masters in Manual therapy recovery strategies and Athletic Performance & Recovery
Consultative Experts. Our services incorporate a holistic and fully encompassing approach to
understand the athlete/individual and provide them with the most effective intervention
strategies to allow them to improve.

Who are our services for

Competitive athletes, keen sports enthusiasts, our services are suitable for all
sports, fitness enthusiasts, gym-goers and the sedentary population.
